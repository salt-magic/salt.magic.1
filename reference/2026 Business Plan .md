To scale nationally, operations are bifurcated to protect brand heritage while maximizing mass-market volume.

* **B2B Sachet Line (Mass Scale):** Contracted to **Di-Well** (Bangkok). A GMP/HACCP certified facility that handles high-speed automated pouch filling. This ensures humidity-proof packaging and the ability to fulfill 10,000+ unit volume spikes for distributors.

* **Raw Materials Risk Mitigation:** We maintain a 3-month buffer stock of Pink Himalayan Salt in Samui to hedge against supply chain delays.

**B. Regulatory & FDA Compliance:** Di-Well is currently finalizing the FDA formulation to ensure Salt.Magic strictly adheres to Thai Maximum Daily Intake limits. Label claims are restricted to "Daily Mineral Balance" and "Water Mineralization" to ensure 100% legal compliance for pharmacy and retail shelves.
