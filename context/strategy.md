## Current Focus Period

**2026 — Full Year** (with THAIFEX in May 2026 as a key inflection point)

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Transition from D2C to National B2B Distribution** — Shift from island-based direct sales to scalable B2B distributor model, bypassing expensive D2C customer acquisition costs
2. **Secure Thai FDA Approval** — Finalize FDA-compliant formulation and labeling with Di-Well to unlock national pharmacy and retail shelves
3. **Land Tier 1-3 Distributor Partnerships** — Execute the "Omni-Tier" B2B acquisition strategy:
   - Tier 1: National giants (DKSH, Central Group) — 30-40% margin at national scale
   - Tier 2: Regional chains (Boots, Lab Pharmacy) — 50-store urban pilot programs
   - Tier 3: Boutique/clinics (Organic Village) — immediate POs for cash flow
4. **THAIFEX May 2026 Execution** — Roaming floor strategy at Asia's largest F&B expo with FDA compliance dossier and TDS Demo pitch tool
5. **Hit 100,000 THB Monthly Net Profit** — Target ~285,000 THB/month gross revenue against ~92,000 THB/month operating burn

## What Success Looks Like

- Thai FDA approval secured and products shelf-ready for national retail
- At least one Tier 1 or Tier 2 distributor signed with confirmed POs
- Single sachet format launched (Q1 2026) and generating impulse-buy revenue
- Monthly net profit consistently at or above 100,000 THB
- Legacy 10% commission "Sunset Clause" executed (April 2026), reverting to 100% house profit
- Strong pipeline of distributor leads generated from THAIFEX

## Key Decisions & Open Questions

- **Final Di-Well packaging costs** will dictate exact wholesale tier pricing — not yet locked
- **VAT threshold (1.8M THB):** When revenue scales, 7% VAT registration kicks in — all MSRPs have a 7% buffer built in, but timing matters
- **Marketing Manager hire (Q3/Q4 2026):** Budgeted at 40,000 THB/month — when to pull the trigger depends on revenue trajectory
- **Single sachet positioning:** Priced 30% above competitors — need to validate this premium holds in pharmacy channel
