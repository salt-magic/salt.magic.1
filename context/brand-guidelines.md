## Mood-Board Analysis (from reference images: MarocMaroc, Panpuri, Alo)

### Overall Aesthetic Direction: "Elevated Natural Luxury"

The three reference brands form a clear triangle:
- **MarocMaroc** = warmth & sensuality (tradition, craft, hammam rituals)
- **Panpuri** = stillness & purity (nature apothecary, minimalism)
- **Alo** = lifestyle aspiration (modern, body-conscious, luxury wellness)

Salt.Magic sits at the intersection: natural ingredients with a luxurious, calm, sensual aesthetic — neither too clinical nor too rustic.

### Visual Themes

- **Nature as stage:** Products in natural settings (beach, stone, forest, linen) — never studio vacuum
- **Calm & ritual:** Every image radiates deceleration and conscious enjoyment
- **Luxury without loudness:** Quality speaks through materials, light, composition
- **Product as protagonist:** Few objects, lots of space, no visual noise

### Color Direction from Mood Board

| Area | Colors |
|---|---|
| Primary | Warm sand/beige, terracotta, amber/gold |
| Secondary | Olive green, muted pink, burgundy/wine |
| Neutral | Cream white (#F5F0E8), warm gray, dark brown |
| Accent | Muted ocean blue, sunset gold |

**Direction:** Warm earth tones dominate. No cold whites, no saturated primary colors. Everything muted, organic, natural.

### Photography Style

- **Light:** Natural light (golden hour, soft daylight, backlight), warm color temperature
- **Composition:** Products on natural textures (stone, sand, wood, moss, linen), shallow depth of field, lots of negative space
- **Mood:** Meditative, still, aspirational but attainable

### Web Design Implications

- **Layout:** Generous whitespace (30-40% breathing room), full-bleed lifestyle imagery, max 2-3 elements per viewport
- **Background:** Warm off-white (#F5F0E8) or soft sand — never pure white
- **Text:** Warm dark brown instead of black
- **CTAs:** Terracotta or burgundy for highlights
- **Imagery:** Products always on natural textures, warm color grading, contextual staging (not white-background cutouts)
- **Animations:** Slow fade-ins, subtle parallax, smooth scroll — nothing jarring
- **Navigation:** Minimalist, sticky header with transparency
- **Overall feel:** Like entering a calm, luxurious spa
