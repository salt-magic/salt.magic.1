## Organization Overview

Salt.Magic is Thailand's premium all-natural, daily electrolyte mineralizer. Founded ~5 years ago, the company solves the "Dead Water" crisis — the widespread consumption of mineral-stripped RO/filtered bottled water (85% of Thai bottled water brands). Salt.Magic turns daily hydration into a clean, three-ingredient mineral ritual designed to be sipped all day.

**Mission:** Re-mineralize water for everyone, not just athletes. Position clean electrolytes as a daily wellness essential across the APAC market.

**Tagline:** "Your body is 70% water. We re-charge it."
