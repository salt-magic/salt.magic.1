Critical Findings
C1 Critical P1 Accessibility Fixed CategoryProof, MarketComparison
Gold text on white/light backgrounds fails WCAG contrast (1.77:1)
text-gold (#D4BFAA) on white backgrounds produces only 1.77:1 contrast ratio, far below the 4.5:1 WCAG AA minimum. Affected: brand stat values ($500M+, 8-figure, $1.4B) and growth percentages (88% on warm-off card).

Before

text-gold → 1.77:1 on white, 1.66:1 on warm-off

After

text-mineral → 8.47:1 on white, 7.9:1 on warm-off
C2 Critical P9 Navigation Deferred PartnerHero
Pitch Deck PDF does not exist - CTA leads to 404
The "Download Pitch Deck" CTA links to /pitch-deck.pdf which does not exist. Leo will provide the PDF later. Link remains as-is until then.
High Findings
H1 High P1 Accessibility Fixed All Partner Sections
No focus-visible styles on any Partner page CTA
All 6 interactive elements (PartnerHero 2x, Why Distribute 1x, inline CTA 1x, PartnerForm 1x) lacked keyboard focus indicators. Nav used focusRing pattern but partner page did not.

Fix applied to all CTAs

focus-visible:outline focus-visible:outline-2 focus-visible:outline-offset-4 focus-visible:outline-gold
H2 High P8 Forms Fixed PartnerForm
Form labels not programmatically associated with inputs
<label> elements had no htmlFor attribute and inputs had no id. Screen readers could not associate labels with their fields.

Fix

htmlFor="partner-name" + id="partner-name" on all 5 field pairs
H3 High P8 Forms Fixed PartnerForm
Form inputs missing name attributes
Without name attributes, form data cannot be serialized or sent to a backend. All 5 fields now have proper names.

Fix

name="name", name="company", name="role", name="email", name="message"
H4 High P1 Accessibility Fixed PartnerHero
Stat labels use text-gold/70 on mineral - contrast ~3.5:1
Gold (#D4BFAA) at 70% opacity on mineral blue (#294B6D) produces ~3.5:1, below 4.5:1 for 12px text.

Before → After

text-gold/70 (~3.5:1) → text-gold (~5.1:1, passes)
H5 High P3 Performance Fixed layout.tsx
Unused font weights loaded (Playfair 700, Inter 300/600)
Brand guidelines specify only weights 400 + 500. Loading unused weights wastes bandwidth. Also removed italic style from Playfair (eliminated in V18).

Before → After

Playfair: ['400','500','700'] → ['400','500'], style: ['normal','italic'] → ['normal']
Inter: ['300','400','500','600'] → ['400','500']
