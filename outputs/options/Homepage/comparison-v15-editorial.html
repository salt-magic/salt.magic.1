Design Decisions

  • White background — solves "copy gets lost in dark background"
  • Salt.Magic as hero card — solves "I don't like the Salt.Magic box compared to competition" (it's no longer equal)
  • No cards on competitors — just text, visually subordinate
  • Competitor verdicts softened — removed "Glorified syrup" (too aggressive for luxury)
  • Gold divider separates hero from alternatives — PANPURI-inspired
  • Playfair Display for numbers + verdict — Grown Alchemist editorial feel
  • All text passes WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1+) on white background
  • Mobile: Hero stats stack vertically, competitors stack as list
  • No WaterBackground animation — clean, fast, accessible
